WHAT ARE WATER BORN DESEASES

Waterborne diseases refer to illnesses caused by the consumption of contaminated water.

Contamination can occur when watersources, such as lakes, rivers, or wells, are polluted

with pathogenic microorganisms or chemical substances.

People can get infected by drinking or coming into contact with contaminated water.
